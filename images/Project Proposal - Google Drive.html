7. ​Introduction: 
Some Preliminary discussion about the thesis/project topics (not more than 1-2 pages). Tuition 
portals are emerging in our country lately. The main reason of this growth is ever increasing 
demand of quality teachers. Students and Parents are seeking the teachers who are reliable, 
potential and less commercial. As we are living in the digital era, internet has become the one stop 
point for most of our demands. So it has became the main media for connecting the qualified 
teachers to the curious students. This project is based on the concept of building the ‘one touch’ 
bridge between teachers & students through internet.
